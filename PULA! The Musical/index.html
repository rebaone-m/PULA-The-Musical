About Us

PULA! The Musical is a premier dance theatre company based in Botswana. Founded in 2010, the company has dedicated itself to celebrating and promoting the rich cultural heritage of Botswana through the powerful medium of musical theatre. "PULA!" means "rain" in Setswana, symbolizing blessings and prosperity, and this theme permeates our productions, bringing stories of hope, resilience, and joy to life on stage.

Our Founder

The company was founded by Andrew Letso Kola, a renowned choreographer and dancer with over two decades of experience in the performing arts. Andrew's vision was to create a platform that not only entertains but also educates and inspires audiences, both locally and internationally.

Artistic Director

Our Artistic Director, Andrew Letso Kola, has been instrumental in shaping the creative direction of the company. With a background in theatre and dance, Andrew brings a unique perspective that blends traditional Botswanan elements with contemporary storytelling techniques.

Core Values

  • Artistic Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in all our productions, ensuring a captivating and professional experience for our audiences.
  • Cultural Integrity: We are committed to preserving and promoting the cultural heritage of Botswana, reflecting the diversity and richness of our traditions.
  • Community Engagement: We believe in the power of the arts to bring people together and foster a sense of community. Our outreach programs aim to make theatre accessible to all.
